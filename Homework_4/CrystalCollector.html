Hollywood Star Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Hollywood Stars below. By clicking on a Star you will add a specific amount of point to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your Result to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Star is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Star.
